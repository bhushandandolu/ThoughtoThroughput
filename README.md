# Studio Tantra — AI Ad Production Platform for Indian Regional Languages

A B2B per-use AI media production platform for Indian SMBs and ad shops. This repository contains strategic documentation, brand guidelines, architecture specs, go-to-market plans, and the landing page for Studio Tantra.

## 📋 Contents

- **`agent_studio_strategy_memo.html`** — Internal strategy memo outlining the business model, customer ICP, pain stack, pricing model, bootstrap costs, and kill criteria. The foundational document for this venture.
- **`01_brand_kit.html`** — Brand identity guidelines, color palette, typography, logo usage, and brand voice specifications.
- **`02_architecture.html`** — Technical architecture documentation covering the 6-agent AI workflow, tech stack, tooling choices, and API integrations.
- **`03_gtm_plan.html`** — Go-to-market strategy, customer acquisition roadmap, launch timeline, and sales motion for the Hyderabad/Bengaluru creative shop ICP.
- **`index.html`** — Public landing page for Studio Tantra with SEO metadata, structured data, and FAQs.

## 🎯 Quick Facts

- **Target Customer:** Creative shops and digital ad agencies (5–25 people) in Hyderabad and Bengaluru
- **Core Offering:** 6-agent AI workflow producing character-consistent ad creatives in regional Indian languages (Telugu, Tamil, Hindi, Marathi, Bangla, Kannada, English)
- **Pricing Model:** Per-asset credits, monthly subscription tiers starting at ₹4,999
- **Delivery:** 18-hour standard turnaround, same-day rush available
- **MVP Target:** 3 unpaid design partners by Month 4, 5+ paying customers by Month 6
