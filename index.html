A six-person ad shop in Hyderabad ships forty creatives a month. Their D2C client just asked for two hundred — in five languages — by Friday.

The traditional path: hire freelancers, juggle voice talent, eat the timeline. Or say no, lose the account. The AI path so far: piece together InVideo, ChatGPT, ElevenLabs, manual editing — and watch character consistency fall apart by scene three.

What's broken today

Three vendors. Four invoices. Six hours of manual consistency fixes per asset.

  • Telugu dub from Fiverr arrives 48 hours late
  • 9:16 / 1:1 / 16:9 each cut by hand
  • Cost per finished asset: ₹3,000–₹15,000
What changes with us

One platform. One invoice. Eighteen hours from brief to ship.

  • Identity Lock keeps your mascot visually stable

§ 05 · Questions

Things people actually ask.

What does Studio Tantra actually do?+
We produce ad creatives using six specialist AI agents. You give us a campaign brief, a brand reference, and a language list. We deliver finished video assets — character-consistent, multilingual, platform-ready — in 18 hours. Per-asset billing, no retainer.
Who is this for?+
Creative shops and digital ad agencies of 5–25 people, primarily in Hyderabad and Bengaluru, serving D2C brands that need regional-language ad creative at scale. We're not currently set up for solo creators, wedding videographers, or full-length film production.
How is this different from Runway, Pika, or HeyGen?+
Those are foundation models or single-purpose tools. We orchestrate them. The work the customer cares about — character consistency across an entire campaign, regional-language dub that doesn't sound robotic, a critique gate that catches drift before delivery — is the workflow on top, not the underlying model. Different problem, different product.
What does it cost?+
Per-asset pricing from approximately ₹250 per finished asset on the Studio tier. Monthly credit packs starting at ₹4,999 for Starter. Final tier confirmed during pilot onboarding — design partners pay nothing for the first 60 days.
Why are you in private beta?+
Because shipping a public product before getting 5–10 partner customers right is how AI products earn refund tickets. We'd rather be slow now and right later. Pilot slots are limited to Hyderabad and Bengaluru ad shops in Q2 2026.
What about copyright and AI labelling?+
Every asset we deliver carries platform-required AI disclosures (Meta, YouTube, Google Ads). We use commercially-licensed APIs only — no scraped training data in our pipeline. Customer assets stay in your account; we don't reuse them for anything else.
